Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to questions you may have in mind

Are your products fresh? ▼
Absolutely! We work with trusted local suppliers and ensure all groceries are stored and delivered under the best conditions.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Paystack, bank transfers, and debit/credit cards. All transactions are secure and protected.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Orders are usually delivered within 24–48 hours. For perishable items, we prioritize same-day or next-day delivery.
Do you charge for delivery? ▼
Delivery fees depend on your location. The exact cost will be displayed at checkout before you confirm your order.
Can I schedule my delivery? ▼
Yes, you can choose your preferred delivery date and time slot at checkout. We'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.
Do you offer bulk purchase discounts? ▼
Yes! Bulk buyers like families, restaurants, or caterers can enjoy special discounts. Contact us for details.
